About Me

Homeschool Philosophy

My father has allowed me the space to discover what my interests are and then use those interests as a launch pad for my education. My homeschooling journey started out rocky but it has since improved.

Since we are not doing traditional school the learning days are not structured and I am allowed a lot of freedom to explore my interests. There was a significant adjustment period where I was always looking for a teacher or adult to tell me what to do and when to do it and at first it was difficult to just do things on my own but after a while I think I have adjusted and now I am much better at being independent.

We spend significant time on foundational subjects like Mathematics, English Language Arts (ELA), and Social Studies and History. We also do Science and other things like physical education.
